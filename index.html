Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Annita's platform and services.

What makes Annita different from other platforms?

Annita is Africa's first truly integrated digital platform that combines e-commerce, fintech, AI, social networking, marketing, logistics, and lifestyle services in one seamless ecosystem. Unlike other platforms that specialize in just one area, Annita provides comprehensive solutions designed specifically for the African market, with localized features and support.

Which countries does Annita currently operate in?

Currently, Annita is fully operational in Liberia, Nigeria, Ghana, and Kenya, with plans to expand to 15 additional African countries by the end of 2024. You can join our waitlist to be notified when we launch in your country.

How secure are transactions on Annita?

Security is our top priority. Annita uses bank-level encryption, two-factor authentication, and advanced fraud detection systems. Our payment processing is PCI DSS compliant, and we regularly undergo third-party security audits to ensure your data and transactions remain protected.

What are the fees for using Annita's services?

Annita offers transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Our e-commerce marketplace charges a small commission on sales (typically 5-10%, depending on product category). Financial services have minimal transaction fees that are significantly lower than traditional banking. Many basic features are free to use, with premium services available through affordable subscription plans.

How can I integrate my existing business with Annita?

Annita provides multiple integration options including API access, CSV imports, and manual setup wizards. Our business onboarding team can help migrate your inventory, customer data, and operations to the platform with minimal disruption. We also offer training and support to ensure a smooth transition.
