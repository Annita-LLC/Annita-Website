Our Journey of Excellence

From our humble beginnings to becoming Africa's leading technology platform, these are the key milestones in our journey.

2021 – The Beginning of a Movement.

Official Launch and Birth of a Vision of Annita!

We Launched Africa’s first all-in-one digital platform for MSMEs—combining e-commerce, fintech, AI, logistics, and more.

Launch
2022 – Building Our Community.

Product Validation & First 1,000 MSMEs Onboarded!

Tested and validated platform with early MSMEs in Liberia; began generating initial revenue and customer feedback for iteration. Within our first year, over 1,000 micro, small, and medium enterprises joined the Annita ecosystem.

Milestone
2023 – Going Continental.

Cross-Border Capabilities Introduced.!

Started onboarding international MSMEs from Ghana, Nigeria, and the diaspora, growing the ecosystem beyond Liberia. Gained strong traction with MSMEs, backed by high satisfaction rates (95%) and rising repeat usage.

Expansion
2024 – Recognized by the Continent.

Top 50 in Africa by African Union!

Recognized among the Top 50 African Startups out of 1,500+ by the African Union Innovation Fellowship.

Award
2024 – Championing Social Innovation.

Orange Social Venture Prize Winner!

Annita wins 1st Place in Liberia from Orange(OSVP), recognized for our innovative and impactful business model.

Award
2025 – MANSA Platform Onboarding!

Onboarded to MANSA Platform!

Annita becomes part of Afreximbank’s MANSA platform, increasing transparency and trust for cross-border trade.

Recognition
2025 – Expanding the Fintech Frontier!

Annitapay Begins Development.

The work begins on building Annitapay, a standalone fintech company connected to the Annita ecosystem.

Product/Fintech
2025 – A Growing Global Family!

3,000+ MSMEs & 1,000+ Global Users.

Our growing community hits 3,000+ businesses and 1,000+ active users across Africa and beyond.

Growth
2025 – Powering Business with Intelligence!

AI-Powered Tools Launched.

We're rolling out AI-driven features to help MSMEs with financial planning, marketing, and operational efficiency.

Milestone
2025 – Reimagining Annita AI!

Started Developing a futuristic AI.

Annita begins a bold new chapter: developing a futuristic AI platform to rival the world's biggest players, designed uniquely for African businesses and creators.

Product/Innovation
